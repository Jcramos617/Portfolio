Hello World

My name is Jordan Ramos and I'm from the Bronx, New York. I am a second-year student at Laguardia community college majoring in New Media digital journalism. I also do digital art on Tumblr with adobe programs including photoshop, illustrator, premiere, after effects and 3D animation with the program Blender. I also like to use WordPress to blog news.


I am a very determined person that wants to show the world that it could be a better place. I feel blue is my color because it is a very calming color to me and shows the peacefulness of the sky especially at night. I also like green because it is the world and nature itself and it shows how beautiful Earth is. I also love 80s rock and grunge rock because it shows how the world could be free-spirited and with grunge doing the same thing, however, it has darker tones and it is really good to listen to.


I'm very inspired by dark comedians such as Dave Chappelle, George Carlin, and Richard Pryor. I also enjoy watching irrelevant movies like the Big Lebowski. I also love sci-fi including Blade Runner which is an inspiration for my artwork. The art reflects on how I feel about the world and how it can be a better place to live in.
